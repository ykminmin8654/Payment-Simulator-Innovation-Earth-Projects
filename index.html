Upcoming Features Beta

These are the features we're building for the first beta release — help us test and improve them!

Multiple Payment Methods

Simulate credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, wallets, and more payment options.

Transaction Analytics

Track and analyze all simulated transactions with detailed reports and logs.

Safe Testing Environment

Test payment flows without using real financial data or making actual transactions.

Customizable Scenarios

Create custom payment scenarios including success, failure, and pending states.

Offline Mode

Simulate payments even without an internet connection for edge case testing.

Export Reports

Export transaction logs and analytics in CSV/Excel format for further analysis.

How the Beta Program Works

Here's what to expect when you join our beta testing program.

1

Submit Your Application

Fill out the beta program application form with your details and experience.

Beta note: Applications are reviewed within 3-5 business days
2

Receive Beta Access

Approved testers get a link to download the beta app and join our feedback channel.

Beta note: Beta app is distributed via Google Play Beta or direct APK
3

Test the App

Use the beta app to test payment simulations and provide feedback on features and usability.

Beta note: We ask for a minimum of 2 hours of testing per week
4

Provide Feedback

Submit bug reports, feature requests, and general feedback through our dedicated channels.

Beta note: Feedback is reviewed weekly by our development team
5

Get Updates

Receive regular beta updates with new features and bug fixes based on tester feedback.

Beta note: Updates are released every 1-2 weeks during beta phase
